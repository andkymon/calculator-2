Keyboard Controls:
  • Numbers & . : Enter digits and decimal
  • + - * / : Operators (× = * or x, ÷ = /)
  • = or Enter : Equals
  • Esc : Clear
  • % : Percent
  • n : Toggle plus/minus
Keyboard Controls: Numbers and period: Enter digits and decimal. Plus, minus, asterisk, slash: Operators (times is asterisk or x, divide is slash). Equals or Enter: Equals. Escape: Clear. Percent: Percent. N: Toggle plus or minus.
